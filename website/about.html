About Clinic-Q

Built with passion for healthcare, designed to make every clinic visit seamless.

Our Story

Clinic-Q was born from the everyday challenges faced at Jivandeep Hospital. Our team witnessed firsthand how long patient wait times, disorganized queues, and manual tracking systems created frustration for both patients and healthcare providers.

In 2024, we set out to build a solution that would bring order to the chaos of outpatient departments. What started as an internal tool for our hospital quickly evolved into a comprehensive OPD management platform used by clinics of all sizes.

Today, Clinic-Q helps healthcare providers across India deliver faster, more organized, and more patient-friendly outpatient experiences. We continue to innovate and improve, driven by our commitment to making quality healthcare management accessible to every clinic.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To simplify and digitize outpatient department operations, enabling healthcare providers to focus on what matters most — patient care. We strive to eliminate inefficiencies and bring modern technology to every clinic.

Our Vision

Making quality healthcare management accessible to every clinic, from small practices to large hospitals. We envision a future where no patient has to wait in uncertainty, and every clinic runs like a well-oiled machine.
